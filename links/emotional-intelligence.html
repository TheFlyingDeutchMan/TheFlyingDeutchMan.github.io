My understanding on it

My understanding of emotional intelligence is that it is the capability to understand and or relate to emotions.
In my case as I lack empathy, I understand from where people come from -- I many not know for how they feel, but in the least I understand them.
Emotional intelligence is also the ability to read peoples expressions and emotions that they have written on their face. A frown can mean distrust, or annoyance
A smile can be simple joy or general happiness. A face scrunched up, can be confusion or disgust.

There are many things that surrounds emotional intelligence, this is what I know and understand of what we call is emotional intelligence.
